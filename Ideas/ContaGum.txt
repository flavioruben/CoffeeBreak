Goal:

Software for managing accounting in Mexico

Features:

- Easy to learn
- Simple UI for the new accounting people
- Information available in the cloud
- Share accounting records between users
- Invite other users to your accounting project
- Integrate a control version
- Login using RFC
- Implement a law of correspondence (in accounting stuff)

Modules:

- Accounting records
- Expenses and incomes
- Paysheet (nice to have)
- Profits and losses
- General balance
- Checking balance
